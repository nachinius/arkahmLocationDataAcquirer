Granny Orne

From Arkham Horror Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Granny Orne is an ally. She first appears in the Kingsport Horror expansion. She can be gained through an encounter at St. Erasmus's Home.

Card info

  • +1 Lore
  • +1 Sneak

Any Phase: Exhaust instead of spending 1 Clue token.


Mythos Source

Granny Orne is mentioned in The Strange High House in the Mist, written by H.P. Lovecraft (1926).
