Citizens Urged to Lock Doors is a mythos card. It first appeared in The Lurker at the Threshold expansion.

Card Information

Mythos Type: Headline

Gate Opens: Gate Burst at Graveyard

Clue Appears At: *Unvisited Isle


Monster Movement
SlashStarTriangle
Hexagon


Mythos Ability: Monsters appear in the Outskirts until the Outskirts limit is reached. If the Outskirts already contains the maximum number of monsters, return all of the monsters in the Outskirts to the monster cup and increase the terror level by 1.
