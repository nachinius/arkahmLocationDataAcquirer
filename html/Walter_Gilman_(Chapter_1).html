Walter Gilman (Chapter 1) is a special ally used in The Dreams in the Witch House of the 1st Arkham Spanish League.

Card info

  • +1 Focus
  • +1 Lore

Investigators lose the game if Walter Gilman is discarded for any reason.

Notes

This is one of multiple versions of the Walter Gilman ally used by different scenarios.
