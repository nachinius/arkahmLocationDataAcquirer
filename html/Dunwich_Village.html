Dunwich Village is a Dunwich location in the Village Commons neighborhood. It first appeared in the Dunwich Horror expansion.

Special Encounter

Osborn's General Store: Instead of having an encounter here, you may pay $4 to roll a die. On a success, draw 1 Unique Item. On a failure, draw 1 Common Item

Walking across the Commons, you pick your way through brambles, shrubs, and other underbrush until you come across a large, charred stump. Sitting down on it for a moment, you are paralyzed as a vision of an angry lynch mob and a defiant victim overwhelms your senses. When you recover, you find that hours have passed. Lose 1 Sanity and stay here next turn. 2
Walking through the town, you are unnerved by the number of hostile stares you receive. However, one young man comes up to you and whispers something in your ear before running off. Gain 1 Clue token. 2
When you sit down at the checkers board, a clearly disturbed stranger sits down opposite you. As you play, he begins telling you about the play he saw, "The King in Yellow." Make a Will (+0) check to maintain your sanity. If you fail, gain a Madness card. Will +0 1011
