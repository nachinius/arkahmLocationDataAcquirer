Thomas F. Malone

From Arkham Horror Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

Thomas F. Malone is an ally. He first appears in the Arkham Horror board game. He can be gained through an encounter at The Witch House.

Card info

  • +1 Fight
  • +1 Lore

Draw 1 Spell when Thomas F. Malone joins you.


Mythos Source

Thomas F. Malone appeared in The Horror at Red Hook, written by H.P. Lovecraft (1925).
