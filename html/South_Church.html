South Church

From Arkham Horror Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

South Church is an Arkham location in the Southside neighborhood. It first appeared in the Arkham Horror board game.

Home of Investigators

  • Sister Mary
  • Mark Harrigan
  • William Yorick

Special Encounter

Blessing: Instead of having an encounter here, you may spend 5 toughness worth of monster trophies or 1 gate trophy to have any investigator you choose be Blessed.
