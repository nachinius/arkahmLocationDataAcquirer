Unvisited Isle is an Arkham aquatic location in the Merchant District neighborhood. It first appeared in the Arkham Horror board game.

Mythos Source

The Unvisited Isle (which has also been referred to as Themystos' Island) was first mentioned in The Colour Out of Space (1927), written by H.P. Lovecraft.
