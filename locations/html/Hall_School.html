The Hall School is a location in the South Shore neighborhood of Kingsport. It first appeared in the Kingsport Horror expansion.

A couple of students have snuck outside, behind the school. Make a Sneak (-2) check to overhear what they say to each other when they think no one else is listening. If you pass, gain 2 Clue tokens. Sneak -2 1211
A glass display case holds various trophies and certificates earned by students over the years. You notice one of the certificates is covered in text written in an ancient language. Make a Lore (-2) check to translate. If you pass, gain 1 Spell. Lore -2 1215

After looking in the school records, you begin to feel a malevolent force has begun to follow you. Make a Will (-1) check to shake the feeling. If you fail, take the Harried card if it is available. Will -1 129

Having gotten permission to look through the archive in the basement, you come across a parchment signed by Eben Hall. Pass a Lore (-1) check to gain 1 Spell. Lore -1 4

Principal Miles invites you to have lunch in the cafeteria. Gain 1 Stamina. 12
Principal Miles is hesitant to allow you in the students' lockers. Make a Will (-2) check to convince him. If you pass, you discover one student's notes on a foray into the arcane arts. Gain 1 Spell. Will -2 12
Principal Miles is looking for guest lecturers. If your Lore is currently 4 or higher, gain a Retainer card. 1213

Principal Miles is very interested in acquiring more books for the library. You may discard 1 Tome in exchange for $2 more than its list price. 1214
Principal Miles shows you the work that the students have been creating in their art class. You can see one student has talent well beyond her years and a strange insight into creatures from beyond. Make a Lore (-2) check and gain 1 Clue token for each success as you examine her work. Lore -2 1215

The further back you look, the more the school's records are incomplete and disorganized, but you find some remarkable things among the paperwork. Draw 2 Spells, choose 1 to keep and discard the other. But the long search results in you being delayed. 1213

The school is selling some of its old texts. You may spend $2 to search the Common Item deck and take the first Tome you find. 12
The students seem all too eager to tell you rumors and ghost stories. Make a Lore (-1) check to discern the useful from the hyperbole. If you pass, gain 1 Clue token. If you fail, you are delayed by the excess of dubious information. Lore -1 12
