Ye Olde Magick Shoppe is an Arkham location in the Uptown neighborhood. It first appeared in the Arkham Horror board game.

Home of Investigators

  • Dexter Drake
  • Lily Chen
  • Akachi Onyele

Special Encounter

Magic Lessons: Instead of having an encounter here, you may pay $5 to draw 2 Spells. Keep one of them and discard the other.
