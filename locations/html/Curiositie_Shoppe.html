The Curiositie Shoppe is an Arkham location in the Northside neighborhood. It first appeared in the Arkham Horror board game.

Home of Investigators

  • Monterey Jack
  • Jacqueline Fine
  • Patrice Hathaway

Special Encounter

Shop: Instead of having an encounter here, you may draw 3 Unique Items and purchase one of them for its list price. Discard the other two items.
