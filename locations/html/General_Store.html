General Store

From Arkham Horror Wiki
Jump to: navigation, search

The General Store is an Arkham location in the Rivertown neighborhood. It first appeared in the Arkham Horror board game.

Home of Investigators

  • Bob Jenkins
  • Diana Stanley
  • Hank Samson

Special Encounter

Shop: Instead of having an encounter here, you may draw 3 Common Items and purchase one of them for its list price. Discard the other two items.
